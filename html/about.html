The Facts.

Present Day Shoe Companies

Currently in the United States there are 927 different Shoe & Footwear manufacturing companies. The most popular being:

  1. Nike
  2. Adidas
  3. Converse

Sponsored Athletes

One of the bigger strategies employed by shoe companies is the sponsorship of professional athletes. They wear the companies shoes and because of that athletes popularity, the company sells more shoes. Here are some of the bigger names that are currently sponsored by a shoe brand:

  • Michael Jordan
  • Neymar Jr.
  • Kris Bryant

Resellers

Not only are shoe companies making a living off of selling their products, but other people are doing it as well. What people are now calling "resellers" are essentially just people who buy exclusive shoes from the shoe companies and then sell them for a higher price when the demand goes up.
